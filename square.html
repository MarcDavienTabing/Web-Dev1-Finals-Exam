2d/3d Shapes

Square
In geometry, a square is a regular quadrilateral. It has four straight sides of equal length and four equal angles. Squares are special cases of rectangles, which have four equal angles, and of rhombuses, which have four equal sides.

Cube
A cube is a three-dimensional solid object and a regular hexahedron, characterized by six congruent square faces, twelve equal-length edges, and eight vertices. It is one of the five Platonic solids and a special case of a rectangular cuboid where length, width, and height are identical.
