2d/3d Shapes

Triangle
A triangle is a two-dimensional, closed geometric shape with three straight sides and three angles. It is the simplest type of polygon, meaning it has the minimum number of sides required to form a closed figure.

Pyramid
A pyramid is a three-dimensional geometric shape (polyhedron) consisting of a polygonal base and triangular lateral faces that converge at a single point called the apex. The number of triangular faces equals the number of sides of the base polygon, and the shape is typically named after its base (e.g., square pyramid, triangular pyramid)
